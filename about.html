About us

We are amongst the leading service providers in the field of security services. Our services are highly acknowledged for authenticity, proper delivery, and timely execution. Offered by us is a spectrum of quality services which encompasses commercial and residential security services, shopping mall security, industrial security, car park security, personal bodyguard services and many more. We render these services up to Six States with PSARA License i.e. Uttar Pradesh, Kolkata, New Delhi, Bihar, Jharkhand & Uttarakhand to different organizations such as corporate business houses, legal agencies, banks, insurance companies, multinationals, co-operative firms, residencies, shopping malls, housing and co-operative societies and other organizations. We have a skilled workforce having in-depth knowledge required to carry out these services to the best of their abilities.
